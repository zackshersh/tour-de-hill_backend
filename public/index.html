The Second Annual

Tour

De Hill

The Tour De Hill is a bike race through the mean streets, sidewalks and corners of the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

Competitors will all begin at the starting line and then must reach the two checkpoints in order.

There is no defined route for the race. Participants may follow whatever route they feel is fastest.

The Route

At both checkpoints competitors will need to stop to be marked or given something to prove they have reached each checkpoint.

Closing Ceremonies

We invite one and all to the closing ceremonies for the second annual Tour De Hill.

Upon completion of the race by all racers we will march in celebration to a yet to be determined location near the finish line.

Songs will be sung, laughter will be had, and the winners will be medalled!

Bikes

Competitors are welcome to bring their own bike or to get one of the Tar Heel Bikes located around campus.

The race will be held in two seperate heats based on bikes with a winner for the normal bikers and one for the Tar Heel bikers.

There is a Tar Heel Bike hub located at SASB. A map can be found with all bike hubs and how many bikes they have here

Rules To Remember:

  • Any sort of bumping is prohibited. There will be multiple bikers going through routes not intended for multiple bikers so be sure to give your competitors adequate space.
  • You may have your mask off for the actual race, but before and during the medal ceremony you must remain masked.
  • Bike safe! Especially on roads, cars still exist and they can still kill you.
